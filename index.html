About Me

A Designer Who
Thinks in Brands

I'm Tejaswi Thakur — a passionate Graphic Designer, Logo Designer, and Brand Identity Specialist driven by the belief that great design is more than aesthetics. It's a feeling, a story, a lasting impression.

With a sharp eye for minimal elegance and a deep love for intentional design, I craft visual identities that don't just look beautiful — they connect. Every curve, colour, and composition I create is rooted in purpose.

I believe brands deserve to be bold, refined, and unforgettable. Whether it's a startup finding its voice or an established business redefining itself, I bring creative thinking and strategic precision to every project.

Featured Work

Projects That
Define My Craft

Children's Brand

GlowBug

GlowBug is a children's lifestyle brand built around wonder, playfulness, and the magic of growing up. The challenge was to create something that resonated with both kids and parents — charming without being childish.

Concept

A firefly-inspired mark symbolising curiosity, light, and the joy of discovery.

Typography

Rounded, friendly letterforms that feel approachable yet polished.

Colours

Warm gold, soft cream, and vivid amber — evoking summer evenings and magic moments.

GlowBug

Firefly • Warmth • Wonder

Pet Care Brand

Mane & Fang

A premium pet grooming and care brand that needed to feel both luxurious and warm. Mane & Fang is for pet parents who treat their animals as family — the brand had to match that devotion.

Concept

A refined monogram weaving animal silhouettes with typographic elegance.

Typography

A bold serif anchors the brand — confident, warm, premium.

Colours

Deep forest green, warm ivory, and gold accents — nature meets luxury.

M&F

Mane & Fang • Premium Pet Care

Jewellery Brand

Twinkle Charm

Twinkle Charm is a boutique jewellery brand specialising in delicate, handcrafted pieces for women who love understated elegance. The brand identity needed to feel precious, feminine, and modern.

Concept

A star-inspired mark with flowing script — capturing sparkle and femininity.

Typography

Thin, elegant script paired with minimal sans — delicate and contemporary.

Colours

Blush pink, rose gold, and soft white — jewel-like yet wearable.

✦ TC ✦

Twinkle Charm • Fine Jewellery

Floral Brand

Bouquet Brand

A luxury floral gifting brand that required an identity as beautiful as the arrangements themselves. The design needed to evoke emotion, romance, and the ephemeral beauty of flowers.

Concept

Flowing botanical illustrations fused with elegant typography create a sensory brand mark.

Typography

High-contrast serif letterforms — classic, emotional, timeless.

Colours

Dusty rose, sage green, and champagne — romance in every shade.
